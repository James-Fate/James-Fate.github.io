Medical Implication

There are a few main symptoms of COVID-19. one being a congested respitory system. This can cause difficulty of breath and make it very difficult to excercise. The next symptom is loss of taste and smell. When infected with COVID-19 is is common that you can't taste any flavors when eating or drinking and you can't smell most aromas. The final symptom we will discuss is the effect on the heart. In some cases COVID-19 has damaged its victims hearts and gave them irregular heart beats and can casue damage to the heart permanently.
